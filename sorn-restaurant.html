Our Story

Rooted in Tradition,
Refined by Passion

"Thai cuisine carries centuries of wisdom — our role is simply to let it speak."

SORN was born from a belief that the richness of Thai culinary heritage deserves a space of quiet reverence. Named after the Thai word for "to teach", every dish here is a lesson in provenance — sourced from small family farms in Chiang Rai, coastal villages in Trang, and heritage paddy fields in Suphanburi.

Our tasting menu changes with the seasons, guided by the rhythms of Thai agricultural tradition. No shortcuts, no artificial intensifiers — only time, technique, and the extraordinary raw materials this land provides.

The Craftsman

Chef Wicha Rattanachot

Executive Chef & Founder

Trained under legends in Lyon and Copenhagen before returning home to Bangkok, Chef Wicha spent a decade travelling every province of Thailand — eating in village homes, learning from grandmothers, and mapping the forgotten spice routes of the kingdom.

At SORN, he synthesises this living archive into menus that feel both deeply familiar and startlingly new. His philosophy: honour the original, then let it breathe.
